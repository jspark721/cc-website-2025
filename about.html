About Us

Over a dinner of crispy chicken sandwiches in 2018, founders Mina Park and Ricardo Marquez made it a mission to build a social-first agency that brought culture to the forefront.

We handpicked a diverse roster of chickens who are obsessed with entertaining the masses while building active communities.
